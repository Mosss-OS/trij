# Trij Demo Video Script — 3 Minutes

**Title:** Offline AI Triage for Community Health Workers — trij Demo

**Setup:** Film on a phone. Airplane mode ON. Show the phone screen clearly.

---

## 0. Opening (15 sec) — Face cam or voiceover

> "This is trij. An offline-first AI triage tool for community health workers in low-connectivity areas. No internet needed. No cloud costs. No patient data leaves this phone."

---

## 1. Landing & Login (20 sec)

*[Show phone opening trij PWA from home screen]*

> "A CHW in a remote village opens trij from their phone home screen. It's a PWA — installed once, works forever offline."

*[Tap through login — show phone OTP or demo mode]*

> "Login takes 30 seconds via phone OTP. Or use demo mode to start immediately."

---

## 2. Capture a Wound Photo (25 sec)

*[Navigate to triage screen. Snap a photo of a printed wound image or your hand with a marker mark]*

> "A patient comes in with a wound. The CHW snaps a photo."

*[Show photo being taken]*

> "That's it. One photo. No forms to fill. No dropdowns. The app guides them."

---

## 3. AI Triage Result (30 sec)

*[Show loading state, then result screen]*

> "The AI runs entirely on-device — Gemma 4 via WebGPU. No data is sent to any server."

*[Show result: e.g. "YELLOW — Urgent. Refer within 24 hours."]*

> "The result comes back in seconds: condition, urgency level — Green, Yellow, or Red — confidence score, and step-by-step management recommendations."

*[Scroll down to show management steps]*

> "Management guidance follows WHO IMCI protocols. Red flag symptoms are detected automatically as clinical safety checks."

---

## 4. Voice Guidance (20 sec)

> "For CHWs with lower literacy, every step has voice guidance in 11 languages — English, Swahili, Yoruba, Hausa, French, Portuguese, and more."

*[Tap the speaker icon, show voice playing]*

> "The CHW can listen instead of read. And they can respond using voice input."

---

## 5. Generate Referral PDF (25 sec)

*[Navigate to referral generation]*

> "If the patient needs referral, trij generates a PDF referral slip instantly."

*[Show PDF preview]*

> "It includes the assessment summary, urgency level, and recommended facility — ready to print or share via WhatsApp when connectivity is available."

---

## 6. Offline → Online Sync (25 sec)

*[Toggle off airplane mode. Show sync indicator]*

> "Every triage is saved to encrypted local storage — IndexedDB with AES-GCM encryption."

*[Show sync completing]*

> "When connectivity returns, records sync automatically to Supabase. The supervisor dashboard updates in real time. Zero data loss."

---

## 7. Close (20 sec)

> "Trij is open source, free for CHWs, and ready for pilot deployment today."

*[Show QR code or link on screen]*

> "I'm looking for 3 pilot partners to deploy to 20 CHWs for 30 days — free, no contract. You'll get a full usage report with accuracy rates and adoption data."

> "Visit [link] or reach out to me directly. Let's put AI triage in every CHW's pocket."

---

## B-Roll / Cutaway Shots (for editing)

- Quick cuts of: patient list, dashboard, multilingual screen, maternal health assessment, IMCI calculator, QR health card sharing
- Map view showing CHW locations
- Supervisor dashboard with analytics

---

## Technical Notes for Recording

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|
| Phone | Android or iPhone, install PWA from browser |
| Mode | Airplane mode (show it on in the first few seconds) |
| Lighting | Natural light, avoid glare on screen |
| Audio | Record voiceover separately for clarity |
| Length | Target 3:00 max. Cut anything that exceeds. |
