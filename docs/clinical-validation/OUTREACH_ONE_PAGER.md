# Trij Clinical Validation Study
## Partner Opportunity: Validating AI-Assisted Triage for Global Health

### About Trij
Trij is an offline-first mobile application that assists Community Health Workers (CHWs) in conducting health assessments using AI-powered image analysis and clinical decision support. Designed for low-resource settings, Trij works completely offline—no server dependency for core functions including image analysis, vital signs assessment, and triage recommendations.

### The Validation Opportunity
We are seeking clinical partners to help validate Trij's AI-assisted triage assessments against clinician-adjudicated ground truth. This prospective study will:
- Measure diagnostic accuracy (sensitivity, specificity, PPV, NPV) across 8 condition categories
- Ensure equitable performance across diverse populations (Fitzpatrick skin tones I-VI, age groups, sexes)
- Generate evidence for potential regulatory pathways and clinical implementation

### Study Design
- **Type**: Prospective, multi-site, observational validation
- **Population**: Patients assessed by CHWs using Trij during routine work
- **Consent**: De-identified data use with explicit patient permission
- **Ground Truth**: Clinician panel review (≥2 physicians per case, adjudication if needed)
- **Target Sample**: 6,000 assessments total (minimum per condition category defined)
- **Duration**: Approximately 12 months per site

### What We Provide Partners
- Complete IRB protocol template (adaptable to local requirements)
- Technical integration guide (Trij works offline; minimal IT burden)
- Data use agreement template
- Training materials for CHWs and site coordinators
- Aggregate performance feedback for your site
- Acknowledgement in all publications and presentations
- Support for manuscript development (sites eligible for authorship)

### What We Need from Partners
- Local IRB/ethics approval (we provide template)
- Identification of CHWs who will use Trij as part of their work
- Clinician reviewers (≥2 licensed physicians) for case adjudication
- Assistance with local consent process (we provide translated templates)
- Minimal site oversight for protocol adherence

### Target Condition Categories
1. Dermatological (wounds, rashes, infections)
2. Respiratory (pneumonia, bronchitis, COPD)
3. Fever/Infectious (malaria, typhoid, UTI)
4. Gastrointestinal (diarrhoea, dehydration)
5. Neurological (stroke, meningitis, neuropathy)
6. Nutritional (SAM, MAM, obesity)
7. Eye/Ear (conjunctivitis, otitis media)
8. Musculoskeletal (fracture, sprain)

### Ideal Partner Institutions
- Hospitals, health centers, or clinics with active CHW programs
- Sites serving diverse populations (important for skin tone stratification)
- Access to laboratory/imaging for ground truth determination (where available)
- Experience with clinical research and IRB processes
- Geographic representation across our target regions
